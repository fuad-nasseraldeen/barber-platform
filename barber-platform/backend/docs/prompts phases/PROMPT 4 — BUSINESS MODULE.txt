Here’s what was implemented:
Business Module
Endpoints
Method	Endpoint	Auth	RBAC	Description
POST	/api/v1/business/create	JWT	-	Create business (user becomes owner)
POST	/api/v1/business/join	JWT	-	Join business with invite token
GET	/api/v1/business/:slug	JWT	business:read	Get business by slug
PATCH	/api/v1/business/:id	JWT	owner, manager	Update business
DELETE	/api/v1/business/:id	JWT	owner	Soft delete business
POST	/api/v1/business/invite	JWT	owner, manager	Invite staff by email
Roles
Role	Permissions
owner	business:manage, staff:manage, user:manage
manager	business:read/update, staff:manage
staff	business:read, staff:read, appointment:read/update, customer:read
customer	business:read, appointment:create/read
Schema
BusinessInvite – businessId, email, roleId, token, expiresAt, acceptedAt
Flow
Create: User creates business → becomes owner.
Invite: Owner/manager sends invite (email + role) → returns invite link with token.
Join: User opens link, calls POST /business/join with token → added as member.
